Creamy Chocolate Fudge

Description

A sweet creamy dessert that is great to share with family and friends.

Made with quality ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. In a saucepan over low heat, combine chocolate chips, condensed milk, and butter. Stir until melted and smooth.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract and salt. Pour into a lined baking dish and refrigerate for 2 hours or until set.
